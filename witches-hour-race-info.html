Course Profile

Elevation by Segment

Mile 0-22: 6,000 ft gain

Mile 22-50: 7,800 ft gain

Mile 50-72: 3,400 ft gain

Mile 72-100: 1,200 ft gain

Terrain Notes

Single-track alpine climbs, rocky ridgelines, and technical descents.

Expect temperatures from 35°F to 80°F.

Aid Stations

Aid stations are positioned approximately every 8-12 miles with full hydration, nutrition, and basic medical support.

  • Start Line · Mile 0 · Full Service
  • Willow Creek · Mile 11 · Full Service + Drop Bags
  • Night Hollow · Mile 22 · Full Service
  • Raven Ridge · Mile 34 · Full Service + Drop Bags
  • Summit Camp · Mile 48 · Full Service + Medical
  • Silver Pines · Mile 60 · Full Service
  • Darkwater · Mile 72 · Full Service + Drop Bags
  • Witching Well · Mile 82 · Full Service
  • Last Light · Mile 92 · Full Service
  • Finish Line · Mile 100 · Food + Gear Recovery

Pacer + Crew Rules

Pacers

Up to 2 pacers total. Pacers allowed after Mile 50 (Summit Camp).

Only one pacer on course at a time.

Crew Access

Crew can access Willow Creek, Raven Ridge, Summit Camp, Darkwater, and Finish Line.

No crew access at Night Hollow or Witching Well.

Crew Parking

Limited parking at Summit Camp. Arrive early and follow marshals.

Race Notes

Expect significant nighttime running with exposed ridgelines. Weather can shift quickly, so pack layers and review the mandatory gear list before arrival.
